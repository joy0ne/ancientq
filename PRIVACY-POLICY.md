# Privacy Policy — AncientQ

**Last updated:** April 2026

## Summary

AncientQ does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Period.

## Data Collection

AncientQ collects **zero** user data:

- **No personal information** — no name, email, phone, or identifiers.
- **No analytics** — no usage tracking, no screen recording, no event logging.
- **No telemetry** — no crash reports, no performance metrics sent externally.
- **No location data** — the app does not request location permissions.
- **No camera or microphone** — the app does not request these permissions.
- **No cookies** — the app does not use cookies or web storage for tracking.
- **No advertising** — the app contains no ads and no ad-tracking SDKs.
- **No third-party SDKs** — no Firebase, Google Analytics, Facebook SDK, or similar.
- **No network requests** — the app operates 100% offline after installation.

## Data Storage

All computation (physics simulations, calculations, text encoding) runs locally on your device. No data leaves your device at any time.

The app does not use localStorage, sessionStorage, IndexedDB, or any persistent storage mechanism for user data.

## Third-Party Services

AncientQ does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or cloud platforms.

If AncientQ is distributed via Google Play, Google may collect device and usage information as described in [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). This collection is performed by the Google Play platform, not by AncientQ.

## Children's Privacy

AncientQ does not collect data from any users, including children under 13 (COPPA) or under 16 (GDPR). The app contains educational scientific content suitable for all ages.

## International Users

Since AncientQ collects no data, GDPR (EU), LGPD (Brazil), CCPA (California), and other data protection regulations are satisfied by default — there is no personal data to protect, transfer, or delete.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be included with the app update and the "Last updated" date will be revised.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the developer through the Google Play Store listing.
